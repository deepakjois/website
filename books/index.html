Books

I will probably never switch to reading books in an electronic format unless they become available in a universally readable format, unencumbered by any sort of DRM and country-specific restrictions. At the same time, I hate buying books because either I do not have the budget for it, or I do not like to accumulate more stuff.

So nowadays, I read books primarily from the library. Sometimes I buy books on the cheap from AbeBooks, which I eventually plan to give away.
